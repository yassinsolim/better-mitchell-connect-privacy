Better Mitchell Connect

Review a locally parsed claim PDF and fill the visible Mitchell Create New Job step.

Focused workflow

The extension adds an Upload claim PDF action inside Mitchell Connect, presents editable Contact, Vehicle, and Insurance values, and fills only after explicit user confirmation. An intake-enabled build also lets authorized Cosmos staff claim a tablet-submitted intake and hand its reviewed values to the same New Job flow.

AI-assisted import

  • Access-controlled publisher services; no analytics or advertising.
  • Extracted PDF text is sent to OpenAI; PDF bytes and structured tablet submissions are not.
  • No automatic Save & Next.
  • Claim drafts use memory-backed session storage; managed intake records follow the privacy policy's deletion schedule.

Beta access

This testing build is limited to approved users with authorized Mitchell Connect access.
